Priorities and issues

Plain-English governance, academic focus, teacher support, and community trust.

Governance & oversight

A school board trustee has a clear job:

  • Hire and evaluate the Superintendent
  • Approve the budget and ensure financial controls
  • Adopt curriculum and set standards
  • Establish policies that govern the district
  • Monitor performance and require accountability

That’s the work I focus on, because governance is how you protect classrooms, support teachers, and prevent waste.

Fiscal accountability

We can invest in teachers and students without losing control of spending. Every program should be measured, and every contract should be defendable.

Academic focus

Strong fundamentals—reading, writing, math—plus pathways that prepare students for college, career, or service.

Support for teachers

Competitive pay is part of the solution. So are working conditions, discipline support, and respect for professional judgment.

Community trust

Parents deserve transparency, consistency, and a board that listens.
